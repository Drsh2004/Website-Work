About Collector Office

The Collector Office plays a pivotal role in the administration of Kolhapur district. It is responsible for law enforcement, revenue collection, disaster management, and public service implementation.

Key Responsibilities

Law & Order

Maintaining peace and ensuring public safety in the district.

Government Schemes

Implementing and monitoring welfare programs.

Revenue Collection

Managing land records and tax collection.

Disaster Management

Coordinating emergency response and relief efforts.
